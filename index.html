About Me!

Hi! My full name is Nathan Alexander Tran, but most of my friends just call me Nate! I am from Vernon Hills, Illinois, which is a northern suburb of Chicago. I was born on September 25th, 2000 in Libertyville, Illinois. I attended Vernon Hills High School and graduated in 2019. I am now enrolled in the School of Information at the University of Michigan. Go Blue!

Since 2015, I have worked as a Youth Lacrosse Coach in my hometown, and while I am not around my hometown as much anymore since I spend the school year in Ann Arbor, I still enjoy coming back to coach in the summer. In high school, I also had a job with 12.23 Lacrosse where I helped maintain their practice fields and helped their coaches run clinics with younger players. This past summer, I had a small remote internship role with a real estate company based out of San Diego. With this company, I helped develop a File Transfer Protocol Website to help them upload and store excel files.
